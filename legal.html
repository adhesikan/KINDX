Token Disclaimer

KINDX token parameters

Ownership of KINDX does not represent ownership, equity, or any financial interest in any company, organization, or project. For Data Providers

KINDX is a decentralized token with no issuer, no profit-sharing mechanism, and no centralized entity providing financial returns. Data providers such as CoinGecko and CoinMarketCap list KINDX for informational tracking purposes only.

Responsible participation
